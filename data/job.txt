Technology Summer Analyst – Example Technology Consulting Firm

Role Overview
We are looking for a Technology Summer Analyst to join our consulting team. As a Summer Analyst, you will help design, build, and test technology solutions for clients across a variety of industries. You will work with project teams to analyze business problems, gather requirements, and implement technical solutions.

Key Responsibilities
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand client requirements and translate them into technical tasks.
• Assist in designing and building software components using languages such as Python, Java, or JavaScript.
• Help integrate systems using APIs, REST services, and JSON-based data formats.
• Support data analysis tasks, including cleaning, transforming, and visualizing datasets.
• Participate in cloud-related work using platforms such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
• Contribute to documentation, testing, and deployment activities.
• Communicate progress, blockers, and results clearly to team members and stakeholders.

Basic Qualifications
• Currently pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Systems, or a related field.
• Familiarity with at least one programming language (Python, Java, C++, or JavaScript).
• Understanding of data structures and algorithms.
• Exposure to databases and SQL.
• Interest in technology consulting, digital transformation, or cloud solutions.
• Strong problem-solving, communication, and teamwork skills.

Preferred Qualifications
• Experience with version control tools such as Git and GitHub.
• Exposure to web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, REST APIs).
• Familiarity with basic cybersecurity concepts, networking, or system administration.
• Previous project work involving automation, data pipelines, or dashboarding.
• Awareness of agile development practices.

What You Will Learn
• How technology consulting projects are run from requirements gathering to deployment.
• Practical experience with real-world client problems and enterprise systems.
• Hands-on exposure to modern tools and platforms used in industry.
• How to work as part of a professional technology team and deliver value to clients.

Location
Houston, TX (with potential hybrid or remote work options depending on team needs).
